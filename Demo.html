Persistently Stuck Chains Demo

This demo replicates the D2C5 condition discussed in our Psychometrika paper, using \( = 2\) (Dirichlet) and Half-Cauchy(5) priors. The example involves 3-class GMMs fit with Stan, 20 chains, and 20000 iterations total (1000 warmup + 1000 post-warmup each).
